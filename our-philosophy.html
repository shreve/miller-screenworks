Inks, Inks, and More Inks.

Most garment printers use inks containing plastics. Those plastic inks work great for the printer but not so well for the customer or the environment.

At Miller Screenworks, we take a different approach. Our inks are all environmentally friendly, certified non-toxic and 100% allergen free. These next generation inks penetrate the individual fibers of the fabric ensuring that the image will last as long as the garment. No more peeling or cracking inks. These inks leave the printed image very soft and smooth and the fabric free to breathe. As no solvents are used in any part of our process, all of our printed garments can be worn right out of the box. No worries about allergens.
