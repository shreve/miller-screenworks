Returns & Exchanges

At Miller Screenworks, customer satisfaction is our priority. Each item is hand printed and inspected during the printing process. Occasionally, flawed garments, misprints, and human errors happen. We do not offer refunds or returns, but if you are unhappy with your purchase, please contact us and we will print you a replacement in exchange.
